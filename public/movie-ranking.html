The best animes according to Devin

My top 3 animes of all time

Full Metal Alchemist Brotherhood

This is my favorite anime with a strong message in morality and the importance of a brotherly bond.

Bleach

This is my second favorite and also has strong messages in morality and perseverance.

Naruto Shippuden

This is definitely a top contender. A strong theme of perseverance and treating others with respect even in conflict.
